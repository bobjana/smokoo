    Smokoo Hot Tips
The most important tip we have is to be patient. Take time to observe both auction and competition!
If you haven’t already – have a read through “A User Guide to Smokoo”
Waiting until the last seconds to bid in an auction will drastically increase your ability to secure an auction!
The AutoBidder used strategically can be used to “scare off” competing bidders. The AutoBidder is great when you still want to participate in an auction but can’t be at your PC to monitor it constantly.
Try and avoid having to top-up your credits during an auction by ensuring that you have enough credits before joining the auction action!
When starting out and developing your own bidding strategy – try BidPackage auctions to boost your credits balance.
Less costly items tend to have slightly less interest and fewer competition – these auctions can usually be secured for fewer bids.
To see details of more than one auction = use the "Open in New Window" option and refresh the Home Page from time to time to see all active and current auctions.
